Following Ministries

Life Groups.

A small community within the big community. The 15 plus life groups meet every week to learn together, to pray together and to fellowship together.

Sunday School.

FCC had a vibrant Sunday School . Children are creatively taught Biblical truths by a team of dedicated teachers who enjoy their task as much as the children.

Jesus and Me.

That’s what the youth wing of FCC is called. Youth love their weekly engagements where they learn together and have fun together as their journey together in their faith walk. The annual summer camps and other activities keep them busy and involved.

Teens Meetings.

The teens are guided by Sameer and Jessie as they go through the most crucial years of their lives.

Ladies and Men’s Fellowship.

The men and women have their monthly rendezvous where they discover themselves and grow in their call. Food and fellow are vital components of these gatherings.

Prayer Initiatives.

The Life Line of FCC: There are many prayer gathering in which the members are involved. Prayer 365 - start the day with Word & Worship , Daily Ladies prayer , Fasting prayer . Leaders Prayer. 

Community Initiatives

Blessed Minds Tuition centre.

Volunteers from FCC help under privileged children to study and do better as they have very less opportunity for academic excellence. They are taught from Tuesday to Friday for a few hours

Tree planting Drive.

FCC along with the Horticulture Dept of Chandigarh planted sapling of - trees. It is a small contribution to stop pollution and care for our environment
